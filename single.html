Non-Profit Cultural Arts Organization Website & Branding Redesign

Teatro De Artes De Juan Seguin - Seguin, Tx
Case Study Updated 12/8/2022

Our research consisted of first analyzing the current aesthetics and function of the current website by conducting heuristic and color accessibility tests. During this testing phase, I was able to connect with the Executive Director Dr. Yvonne De La Rosa for an interview about the history of this organization and the imprtance of their online presence.

The following day, my team listened to the recorded interview together and we then started to construct an affinity diagram using the data collected. We decided to form the on-hand data into an empathy map to better reflect the organization's needs. As a team, we felt a User Persona will be more beneficial after actual interviews with current Teatro families.
